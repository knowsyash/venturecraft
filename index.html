Our Vision

Venture Craft 2025 is the flagship entrepreneurial event of Oriental College of Technology, Bhopal. It is designed to ignite innovation, creativity, and startup culture among students. The event provides a platform for budding entrepreneurs to present their ideas, compete, and gain recognition from industry experts.

Anticipated Participation:
  • 700–800 student participants (teams and individuals)
  • 7000+ audience (students, faculty, professionals, and guests) from multiple colleges across the region

About Us

VentureCraft is not just an event; it’s a platform where ideas take shape, innovations find direction, and aspiring entrepreneurs turn dreams into reality.

With the vision of inspiring creativity and cultivating an entrepreneurial mindset, VentureCraft brings together students, innovators, and changemakers who dare to think differently. The event celebrates the spirit of entrepreneurship, offering participants the opportunity to present their startup ideas, pitch to experts, and showcase their ability to solve real-world challenges through innovation.

This year’s theme, “Igniting the Entrepreneur Within,” focuses on empowering young minds to explore the limitless world of startups from identifying opportunities to crafting business models that make an impact.

Through engaging sessions, interactive discussions, and idea presentations, VentureCraft creates an environment where learning meets leadership and imagination meets execution.

Whether you’re a budding entrepreneur with a game-changing idea or someone curious to understand the world of startups, VentureCraft is your stage. It’s where collaboration thrives, creativity flows, and visions turn into ventures.

Join us on 27th & 28th November as we embark on this exciting journey of ideas, innovation, and inspiration because the future belongs to those who craft it.
